How Swasthya Sampark Works

A seamless connected ecosystem designed to protect you from registration to emergency response.

1️⃣

1. Generate Health ID

Register online to get your unique QR Code. Your entire medical history is encrypted and linked to this digital ID.

2️⃣

2. Instant Doctor Access

Doctors scan your QR to instantly access your history, add prescriptions, and run AI health-risk predictions.

3️⃣

3. AI Emergency SOS

In a crisis, tap the SOS button. Our Machine Learning engine automatically prioritizes your ambulance dispatch based on urgency.

Have questions about features or general health guidelines? Our intelligent Swasthya Sampark Assistant is available globally to guide patients, doctors, and hospitals.
